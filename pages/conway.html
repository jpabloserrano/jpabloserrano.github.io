Conway's game of life

Conway's game of life is a zero-player game devised by the mathematician John Conway. It is a Turing complete cellular automaton.

In this page, you can find out how Conway's game of life is played

To start playing, click the screen below to turn on a cell alive. Press ENTER to start the game and to finish it.
